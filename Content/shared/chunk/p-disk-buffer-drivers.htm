The following destination drivers can use disk-based buffering: amqp() , elasticsearch2() , file() , hdfs() , http() , kafka() , mongodb() , program() , redis() , riemann() , smtp() , snmp() , sql() , stomp() , unix-dgram() , and unix-stream() . The network() , syslog() , tcp() , and tcp6() destination drivers can also use disk-based buffering, except when using the udp transport method. (The other destinations or protocols do not provide the necessary feedback mechanisms required for disk-based buffering.)
